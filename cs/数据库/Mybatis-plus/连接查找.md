#MybatisPlus 

`fullJoin` 必须完全相同
`leftJoin` 可以为空

```java
MPJLambdaWrapper<BookChapterTab> wrapper = new MPJLambdaWrapper<BookChapterTab>()  
        .selectAll(BookChapterTab.class)  
        .select(SysCommonLanguageTab::getLanguageContent)  
        .fullJoin(SysCommonLanguageTab.class, w -> w  
                .eq(SysCommonLanguageTab::getEntityName, BookChapterTab.class.getSimpleName())  //这两个相同
                .eq(SysCommonLanguageTab::getEntityKeyId, BookChapterTab::getChapterId)  //这两个相同
                .eq(SysCommonLanguageTab::getLanguageId, "en")  //这两个相同
        )  
        .eq(!ObjectUtils.isEmpty(qo.getCollectionId()), BookChapterTab::getCollectionId, qo.getCollectionId())  
        .eq(!ObjectUtils.isEmpty(qo.getChapterId()), BookChapterTab::getChapterId, qo.getChapterId())  
        .like(!ObjectUtils.isEmpty(qo.getChapterName()), SysCommonLanguageTab::getLanguageContent, qo.getChapterName())  
        .orderByAsc(BookChapterTab::getSort)  
        .orderByDesc(BookChapterTab::getCreatedAt);  
IPage<BookChapterListVO> result = bookChapterMapper.selectJoinPage(qo.getPage(), BookChapterListVO.class, wrapper);
```
